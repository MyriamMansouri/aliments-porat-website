Fresh Pesto Dip

Preparation: 5 min

This dip has a summery taste. All the more reason to serve it in any season! It goes perfectly with raw vegetables, pita bread or nachos.

Ingredients

  • 1 Végé Pâté Pesto Porat
  • Silky tofu and/or vegan mayo
  • 1 Green Pepper
  • 1 Fresh Tomato
  • Chili powder

Preparation

  1. In a bowl, mix the Végé Pâté Pesto with the silky tofu and/or the vegan mayo.
  2. Add finely chopped pieces of peppers and tomatoes.
  3. Add a pinch of chili to taste.
