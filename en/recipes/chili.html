Vegan chili

Preparation: 15 min Cooking time: 28 min Portions: 4

We borrowed this recipe from our mom, founder of Porat Natural Food and a great cook. A recipe that warms the heart and the body is a must in the family cookbook.

Ingredients

  • 2 Veggie Burgers Original (1/2 package)
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • 1/2 lb tofu
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 1 zucchini
  • 1 can (796 ml) of stewed tomatoes
  • 1 can (398 ml) tomato sauce
  • 2 cans (540 ml) of kidney beans
  • 5 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1½ tsp brown sugar
  • 1½ tsp red wine vinegar

Preparation

  1. Dice burgers, tofu, bell pepper and zucchini. Mince the garlic and chop the onion.
  2. In a frying pan, lightly brown the tofu and set aside.
  3. Heat the oil in a large thick-bottomed pan.
  4. Sauté the onion over low heat for 2 minutes.
  5. Add garlic and cook another minute.
  6. Increase the heat, then add the green bell pepper and zucchini. Cover and cook for 5 min.
  7. Add the stewed tomatoes, tomato sauce, kidney beans, veggie burgers, spices and brown sugar. Cover and simmer for 20 min.
  8. Turn off the heat and stir in the vinegar. Yum, it's ready!
