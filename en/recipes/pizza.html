Cajourella pizza

Preparation: 15 min Cooking time: 15 min Portions: 4

As vegans, one thing we miss a lot is pizza. Our very passionate love for this dish led to the creation of Cajourella, our cashew cheese. This combination between Cajourella and pizza dough is extraordinary. It is this recipe of pure joy that we want to share with you.

Ingredients

  • 1 Porat Cajourella
  • 4 Porat Veggie Burgers Original (1 package)
  • 1 pizza crust
  • Pizza sauce
  • Fresh cherry tomatoes
  • 1/2 red onion
  • Pitted kalamata olives
  • Arugula

Preparation

  1. Preheat the oven at 375˚F.
  2. Cut the cherry tomatoes in half and slice the onion.
  3. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the crust.
  4. Add onions, cherry tomatoes and olives.
  5. Break the burgers by hand into small, even pieces and place them on the pizza.
  6. Melt the cheese in a saucepan over low heat, stirring continuously.
  7. Spread the melted cheese on the pizza.
  8. Bake the pizza in the oven according to the crust’s instructions.
  9. Add the arugula on top. Enjoy!
