Our Products

Our thinking at Porat has always been to make the products we would have liked to find in grocery stores. That's why we have developed healthy and tasty foods. Our products require little preparation and are very easy to incorporate into meals.

We offer several ranges of products, for you to enjoy:

  • Our burgers
  • Our pâtés
  • Our cretons
  • Our Cajourella cheese

OUR BURGERS

Our burgers are made with organic tofu and millet and are an excellent source of protein. Prepare them as a traditional burger or enjoy trying out some of your favorite recipes, whether it be spaghetti sauce or a yummy chili.

Original

Ingredients:
Organic tofu (soybeans, water, magnesium chloride), organic brown rice flour, vegan "mozzarella" (soy beverage, water, tapioca starch, salt, soy lecithin, agar agar), organic soy flour, millet, gluten-free organic tamari (water, organic soy, salt, alcohol, aspergillus oryzae, bacterial culture), yeast extract, canola oil (GMO-free), sea salt, spices, herbs, garlic powder, onion powder.
The original burger is made without wheat (may contain gluten).
May contain nuts, wheat, sesame seeds and sunflower seeds.

Bacon

Ingredients: Organic tofu (soybeans, water, magnesium chloride), stone-ground whole wheat flour, vegan "mozzarella" (soy beverage, water, tapioca starch, salt, soy lecithin, agar agar), organic soy flour, millet, onions, canola oil (non-GMO), sea salt, vegan bacon flavour (autolyzed yeast extract, maltodextrin (corn), salt, smoke flavour, silicon dioxide (conditioning agent)).
May contain nuts, sesame seeds and sunflower seeds.

Sesame

Ingredients: Organic tofu (soybeans, water, magnesium chloride), stone-ground whole wheat flour, organic soy flour, millet, onions, organic sesame seeds, tahini, organic gluten-free tamari (water, organic soybeans, salt, alcohol, aspergillus oryzae, bacterial culture), yeast extract, canola oil (GMO free), sea salt, spices, herbs, garlic powder, onion powder.
May contain nuts and sunflower seeds.

OUR PÂTÉS

Whether it be on a good bread, crackers or sandwiches you will find multiple occasions to enjoy this spread made with chickpeas, sunflower seeds and organic tofu. Made without wheat (may contain gluten).

Traditional

Ingredients:
Purified water, organic tofu (soybeans, water, magnesium chloride), chickpeas, sunflower seeds, canola oil (GMO free), torula yeast, organic brown rice flour, organic gluten free tamari (water, organic soy, salt, alcohol, aspergillus oryzae, bacterial culture), potato flour, yeast extract, onion powder, vinegar, sea salt, carrageenan, fair trade organic raw cane sugar, herbs, garlic powder, spices, citric acid. May contain nuts, sesame seeds and wheat.

Mediterranean

Ingredients: Purified water, organic tofu (soybeans, water, magnesium chloride), diced tomatoes (tomatoes, tomato juice, salt, citric acid and calcium chloride), organic sundried tomatoes, chickpeas, canola oil (non-GMO), sunflower seeds, tomato paste, organic brown rice flour, potato flour, vinegar, spices, fair trade organic raw cane sugar, nutritional yeast, dehydrated red peppers, sea salt, carrot powder, beet powder, citric acid, agar agar.
May contain nuts, sesame seeds and wheat.

OUR CAJOURELLA CHEESE

Cajourella is a cheese made with organic cashew nuts and organic coconut oil, mozzarella di bufala style. We have created this cheese with a very simple objective: to offer the vegan cheese that we, at Porat, have always dreamed of eating. We love it as grilled cheese, au gratin on a lasagna or a delicious pizza . If you are looking for simplicity, Cajourella on a slice of fresh bread is your best friend.

Cajourella

Ingredients: Water, organic coconut oil, quinoa rejuvelac, organic cashew nuts, tapioca starch, carrageenan, sea salt, GMO-free soy lecithin.
May contain wheat.
